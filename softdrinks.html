ABOUT THE SOFT DRINKS

A soft drink is a beverage that typically contains water (often, but not always, carbonated water ), usually a sweetener and usually a flavoring agent. The sweetener may be sugar, high-fructose corn syrup, fruit juice, sugar substitutes (in the case of diet drinks) or some combination of these.
